The Charter

Four requirements. All earned, none granted. Verifiable by anyone.

When granted, it comes with a cryptographic attestation anyone can check on the verify page: the charter text hashes to the attested value or it doesn't.

The Charter unlocks when all four are met. No shortcuts — that's the point of it.
