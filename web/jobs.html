  1. Free Google key (no credit card, ~30s) — open aistudio.google.com/apikey → Create API key → copy it. On the Playground, the provider is already set to Gemini (free) — paste the key once and every tool runs. Generous free tier, no card ever.
  2. No key at all — on the Playground, switch the provider to In-browser. A small model runs entirely on your device — private, free, nothing to sign up for. (Lower quality than the hosted models, but great for a first try.)
  3. Use free Claude.ai — on any skill, click “Copy for Claude”, then paste it into a free claude.ai chat. Real Claude, no API key.

Prefer your own Claude / OpenAI key? Just pick it in the provider dropdown. Your key stays in your browser — never sent to us.
