The Firm

Don't run tools. Employ a staff.

🔒 Everything — charter, memos, minutes, predictions — lives in this browser. Sent only to the model provider you choose, only when you convene.

Every other page is pull — you show up and run a tool. The Firm is push: charter a standing staff once, and each time you convene, every member files a delta-aware memo on their beat (what changed, not what they said last time), ending in a falsifiable prediction. Then the staff hold a boardroom session without you and send you the minutes — decisions, dissent preserved. Score the predictions as reality lands, and watch which of your staff has earned trust.
