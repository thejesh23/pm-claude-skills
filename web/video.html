Video export

Any output → a short animated clip you can post.

🔒 Rendered entirely in your browser (canvas + MediaRecorder). Nothing is uploaded. Uses your Brand Kit colour if set.

Paste a pitch, a one-pager, a launch brief, or any skill output. The renderer pulls the headline, the big numbers, and the key takeaways and turns them into a **motion graphic** — counting figures, a soundtrack, your brand colour — as a .webm you can drop on LinkedIn or X.
