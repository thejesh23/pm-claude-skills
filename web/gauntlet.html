Paste the job description. You'll get what they *actually* want, the interview loop to expect, and your angle.

⚒️ Stage 2 — Forge your stories

Give 2-3 real experiences (messy notes fine). They come back as tight, metric-led stories mapped to what Stage 1 said this role tests.

🎤 Stage 3 — The interview

The hiring manager has read the posting and your stories. Five questions, one at a time. Honest "I don't know" beats a bluff.

💰 Stage 4 — The offer

You got it. Now the recruiter calls with a number — and a hidden approval band. Negotiate; close or push. 4-6 exchanges is realistic.
