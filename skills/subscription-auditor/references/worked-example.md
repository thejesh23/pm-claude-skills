## Why it's shaped this way

- **13 months scanned, and it mattered** — the DesignSuite annual ambush only appears beyond the 12-month window; it's also the single largest decision on the page.
- **Everything is annualized next to the monthly** — $34.99 anesthetizes; $419.88 cancels itself.
- **DesignSuite gets "Confirm," not "Cancel"** — one evidence signal short, so the verdict is a question with a deadline, per the evidence bar.
- **The household overlap is half the findings** — the Duo double-pay and the fitness redundancy; single-person audits miss the biggest line.
- **AudioTales gets Pause, not cancel** — verdicts serve the user's value (9 credits), not the cancellation count.
- **Retention offers are pre-framed** ("50% of unused is 100% waste") — the phone script is armor for the moment the portal fights back.
